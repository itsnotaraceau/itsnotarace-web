Why Your Support Matters

It's Not A Race is a collective of content creators who want fossil fuels out of politics. Donate today to help us make content to overcome misinformation and help it spread to everyone who needs to see it.​

Because the Australian Government doesn't like criticism, It's Not A Race has to abide by a bunch of election regulations. We can only accept donations from Australian citizens, permanent residents and Australian companies. It's Not A Race is considered a political campaigner under the new (very broad) definition, and so is required to disclose the name and address of any person or entity that makes donations above the $16,900 disclosure threshold (this financial year) to the Australian Electoral Commission.
